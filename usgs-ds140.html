What was ingested

Every numeric cell through 1992 was machine-extracted from the official XLSX workbooks listed in the USGS Data Series 140 catalog. The project preserves the workbook, worksheet, header, year, value, published unit statement, price basis, and missing-cell counts.

No worksheet series is silently combined with another. No project interpolation, deflation, aggregation, or unit conversion is applied. USGS notes that its historical series may include agency-standardized conversions and estimates; consult each workbook’s embedded notes before formal citation.
