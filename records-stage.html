Trust architecture

Built for citation, not assertion

Metadata-only by design

The browser cache contains dates, titles, summaries, subjects, identifiers, source URLs, citations, and caveats. Full FRUS, NARA, report, cable, and publication text remains at the authoritative source.

Source hierarchy

FRUS, NARA, Census, USGS, State, DLA, DOE, Federal Register, and other official U.S. sources are preferred and labeled.

Proxy discipline

HS codes describe traded products and baskets, not necessarily mined origin. Query details and caveats must travel with every trade observation.

Visible uncertainty

Placeholder searches, historical geography, and unverified report claims are marked as research needs rather than blended with verified evidence.
